Dataset

Download the dataset


Metadata

geographic_country: The specific countries or regions under consideration.

disturbance_type: Divided into two crucial categories - urbanization and agriculture. Each entry in the dataset is associated with either urbanization or agriculture, providing a clear distinction between these two crucial types of disturbances.

geographic_continent: specifies the geographic continent associated with each country in the dataset.

Protocol

Data quality check:

  • Examine the dataset for outliers and missing values
  • Address missing values and outliers through proper measures

Country selection:

  • Filter out non-African countries based on the column ‘geographic_continent’.

Column filtration:

  • Remove unnecessary columns

Filter the year:

  • For consistency, we focused our analysis on the years 1990 to 2019, ensuring a standardized timeframe throughout our study.

Group by the disturbance type column:

  • We organized the data by grouping African countries based on their disturbance types, creating a dataset that pairs countries with their respective disturbance categories.
